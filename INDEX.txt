================================================================================
🧬 ALGORITHMS
================================================================================

SGATS (State-aware Guided Automatic Test Suite)
  • Reduces paths: 80-90%
  • Preserves coverage: 95%+
  • Complexity: O(n² log n)

EvoPath-GA (Evolutionary Path Genetic Algorithm)
  • Additional reduction: 33-50%
  • Multi-objective fitness
  • 40-60 generations typical

Mutation Testing
  • 6 operator types (AOR, ROR, LOR, SDL, RET, CON)
  • Quality score: >90% target
  • Automated fault injection
